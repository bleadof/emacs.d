  `M-x describe-personal-keybindings`
* Show you the keybindings for a command
  `C-h f`
  `M-x describe-function`
* Show all keybindings
  `C-h b`
  `M-x describe-bindings`
* List all keybindings by mode
  `C-h m`
  `M-x describe-mode`
